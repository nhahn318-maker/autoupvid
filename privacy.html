Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2026

Auto Vid helps creators prepare short-form videos and manage publishing workflows. This policy explains the limited information used by the app.

Information We Use

When a creator connects a TikTok account, Auto Vid may request basic profile information such as an open ID, display name, and avatar. The app may also process videos and metadata that the creator selects for upload.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to authenticate the creator, display the connected account in the dashboard, and submit creator-approved video publishing requests.

Sharing

Auto Vid does not sell personal information. Information is shared with TikTok only when needed to complete the creator-authorized login or publishing action.

Data Retention

Local project files and publishing records are controlled by the creator. Creators can remove local files or disconnect access at any time.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner through the GitHub repository: Auto Vid.
